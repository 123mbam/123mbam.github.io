Meryam Bukhari is a Product Leader in Google's ads business.

I have contributed to YouTube, Display, and privacy efforts in Android. I partner cross-functionally to solve problems that make ads better for our advertisers. I have launched optimization features to grow supply, price effectively, bid for more online conversions, improve auction dynamics, and privacy proof conversion measurement. I was awarded a top ranked PM designation in the company twice and opportunities for L&D and mentorship for future leaders.

Before Google, I worked on smaller adtech and started my full-time career at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York as a Rotational Analyst. After learning, conducting research, and driving a technology transformation program under the mentorship of brilliant central bankers, I graduated the rotational program early. I have earned my MBA from MIT's Sloan School of Management and my BA with honors from Hunter College.
