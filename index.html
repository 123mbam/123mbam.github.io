I am a Product Leader in Google's ads business, where I have contributed to YouTube, Display, and privacy efforts in Android. I partner cross-functionally to solve problems that make ads better for our advertisers. In the past, I have launched optimization features to grow supply, price effectively, bidding, auction, and conversion measurement.

Before Google, I worked in smaller adtech companies and started my full-time career at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York as a Rotational Analyst.
